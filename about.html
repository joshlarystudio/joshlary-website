Josh Lary is an artist based in Los Angeles, CA, with a BFA from Ringling College of Art & Design. His work is deeply influenced by his journey in sobriety and introspection into actions, behaviors, and thought patterns.

He says, “The selfish behavior I used to cope with life was the wallpaper to much deeper issues. I create art to help inspire change, new perspectives, and deeper self-analysis.”

Josh’s work is home to many personal commissions and has been displayed in several galleries across Los Angeles, San Francisco, Stockholm, and Sarasota.
